About us

Trigon Blockchain is a cutting-edge Ethereum Layer 2 solution leveraging Celestia as its data availability source. It offers incredibly low fees, making it highly accessible for both developers and everyday users. With its innovative architecture, Trigon ensures lightning-fast transaction speeds, typically completing in under a second. By combining Ethereum’s robust ecosystem with Celestia’s efficient data layer, Trigon delivers unparalleled scalability and performance. Its seamless design caters to decentralized applications, empowering developers to build without worrying about high costs or delays. Whether you’re deploying smart contracts or executing simple transactions, Trigon provides a smooth, cost-effective, and reliable blockchain experience.

Our features

Trigon Blockchain’s DEX offers ultra-fast, low-cost trading powered by its Ethereum Layer 2 design and Celestia’s data availability. Transactions confirm in under a second, with minimal fees for users and developers. The DEX supports seamless swaps, high liquidity, and secure trading, making it perfect for effortless DeFi use.

The Trigon Wallet is a secure, browser-based wallet designed for seamless interaction with the Trigon Blockchain. Available on Google, it keeps private keys safely stored within the user’s own browser, ensuring maximum security. The wallet supports multiple Trigon RPCs and all ERC20 tokens, making it versatile for managing assets, interacting with dApps, and performing transactions quickly and efficiently.

The Trigon Name System (TNS) is under active development, allowing users to purchase unique usernames now. While these usernames cannot yet be used to send or receive funds, this functionality is being updated and will soon be available. Built on Trigon’s fast and low-cost infrastructure, TNS will make blockchain interactions more intuitive and user-friendly.

Trigon Blockchain supports bridged USDC, known as USD.e, enabling seamless transfers of stablecoins across networks. USD.e brings the stability of USDC to Trigon’s fast and cost-efficient ecosystem, allowing users to enjoy near-instant transactions with minimal fees. Whether for trading, payments, or DeFi, USD.e ensures a reliable and scalable stablecoin experience.
